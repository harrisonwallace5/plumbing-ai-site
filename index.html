How It Works

Jackson Plumbing Plan AI is designed for fast evaluation of residential and commercial floor plans, with internal model processing and email-based delivery.

1

Step 1 — Upload

Submit your floor plan PDF or image through our secure demo request form.

2

Step 2 — AI Processing

Our proprietary TinyUNet model identifies fixture locations, applies probability-guided pipe routing, and generates WSFU (Water Supply Fixture Unit) schedules.

3

Step 3 — Delivery

Receive your annotated plan package via email, typically within 1 business day.

Powered by TinyUNet — a lightweight convolutional architecture trained on thousands of residential and commercial floor plans.

  • Probability-guided pipe routing
  • WSFU scheduling per IPC/UPC standards
  • Fixture detection with >90% accuracy
  • Hot/cold line differentiation
