Privacy Policy

Jackson Plumbing Plan AI uses internal AI processing to evaluate submitted floor plans and return plumbing permit package materials by email.

Effective Date: June 2025

What We Receive

We receive floor plan images and PDFs, including JPG, PNG, and PDF files, along with the requestor's name, email address, and company name.

How We Use Submitted Materials

We process submitted materials with AI to generate annotated plumbing drawings, WSFU fixture schedules, and pipe routing schematics. Results are returned by email as part of a demo workflow.

Data Retention

We do not store floor plans beyond 24 hours after delivery. Contact information is retained only as needed to follow up on the demo request unless an opt-out request is made.

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing.

No Direct Model Access

There is no direct public access to the model. This is a demo-only workflow, and results are delivered by email.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect submitted files and contact information.

California Privacy Rights

California residents may request information about the personal data we collect, request deletion, and exercise any applicable privacy rights. We do not sell personal data.

GDPR and UK Rights

Users in the EU and UK may request access, rectification, erasure, or portability of their data, subject to applicable law.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by Texas law, and any disputes will be resolved in Travis County, Texas.
