Privacy Policy

Jackson Plumbing Plan AI receives project details and floor plan files only for evaluating requests and returning plumbing package outputs by email.

Effective Date: June 2025

What We Collect

We receive floor plan images and PDFs in formats including JPG, PNG, and PDF, along with your name, email address, and company name.

How We Use It

We process submitted files with AI to generate annotated plumbing drawings, WSFU fixture schedules, and pipe routing schematics.

Data Retention

We do not store floor plans beyond 24 hours after delivery. Contact information is retained only for project follow-up unless you request opt-out or deletion.

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

No Direct Model Access

There is no direct access to the model. The service is demo-only, and results are delivered by email.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect uploaded documents and contact information.

CCPA

California residents may have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal data.

GDPR

EU and UK users may request access, rectification, erasure, or portability of their personal data.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by Texas law, and disputes will be resolved in Travis County, Texas.
